Web Design for Korean Restaurant

About The Company

Noona jib in Korean means Big Sister's House which means a comfortable restaurant and delicious food, like playing at your sister's house. NoonaJib Korean restaurant in the Pererenan area.

The Goal

This would be their first proper website, so they wanted this website to convey their needs:

  1. As a central hub for information about their company and products.
  2. Can reserve a place via the website
  3. There are story posts like Instagram that always update about the company
